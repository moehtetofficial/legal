1. What Data We Store

When you connect your Facebook account to Moe AI Tools, we may store:

  • Your Facebook user ID, name, and profile picture URL
  • Your email address (if granted)
  • Access tokens for Pages you have authorized
  • Content you have scheduled or published through the App
  • Usage logs and timestamps related to your account

2. How to Request Deletion

You have two options for requesting deletion of your data:

Option A — Email Request

  1. Send an email to myat.mm65@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: DATA DELETION REQUEST
  3. Include the email address associated with your Facebook account
  4. Include your Facebook user ID if you know it (optional)
  5. We will respond within 7 business days and complete deletion within 30 days

Option B — Revoke Through Facebook

  1. Go to facebook.com/settings → Business Integrations
  2. Find Moe AI Tools in the list of connected apps
  3. Click Remove to revoke all permissions
  4. Facebook will send us a deletion callback, and your data will be removed from our systems within 30 days

3. What Gets Deleted

Upon a valid deletion request, we permanently delete:

  • Your profile data and Facebook user ID
  • All stored access tokens for your Pages
  • Any content drafts or scheduled posts tied to your account
  • Usage logs that identify you personally

4. What May Be Retained

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that no longer identifies you, and any records we are legally required to keep (e.g., for tax or fraud-prevention purposes). Public content already published to your Facebook Page remains under your Page's control — to remove that content, delete it directly on Facebook.

5. Confirmation

Once deletion is complete, we will send a confirmation email to the address on file. If you do not receive confirmation within 30 days, please contact us again.
